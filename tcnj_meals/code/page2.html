We accept any and all donations. Please see below for examples of popular donations that are extremely useful.

Examples of Donations

Food Related
  • Juice (Large bottles or kids size)
  • Bottles of water
  • Baby Milk Formula
  • Baby Food
  • Canned Soups
  • Canned Vegetables
  • Canned Protein (Tuna, Chicken, etc)
  • Cereal
  • Pasta
  • Jars of peanut butter
  • Jars of jelly
  • Already made peanut butter and jelly sandwiches
  • Snacks (chips, granola bars, etc)

Toiletries
  • Baby Diapers
  • Sanitary Napkins
  • Shampoo, Conditioner
  • Bars of Soap, Body Soap
  • Lotion
  • Toothpaste, Toothbrushes
  • Shaving Cream and Razors

Other Items
  • Clothes
  • Winter Necessities (winter coats, socks, gloves, etc)
  • Business Wear

Where can I drop off these items?

They can be dropped off at any time we are open! If you need assistance, we are more than happy to coordinate a time and location to get the donations. Please call us at (908) 239-6308 or email us at tcnjmeals@gmail.com.
